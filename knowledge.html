02

The Caveats of Registration

"A Registered Company is a legal person—and like any person, it must follow the law."

Critical Warning

Non-compliance is not just a fine; it’s a career-ender. Directors can be disqualified for 5 years, and bank accounts can be frozen instantly.

  • The Identify Check: You need a DIN (Director Identification Number) and a DSC (Digital Signature) before you even begin.
  • Lifting the Veil: If you use your company for fraud, the "Limited Liability" shield is broken, and your personal assets (car, home) can be seized.

The "Piercing the Veil" Concept

Young founders often believe that registering a "Private Limited" company protects their personal bank accounts from all business losses. **This is a half-truth.** If you fail to file annual returns for two consecutive years or use business funds for personal luxury, the law can "pierce the corporate veil," making the Directors personally liable for every rupee of debt.
