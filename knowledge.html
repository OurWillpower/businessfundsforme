Risk Disclosure

Piercing the Corporate Veil.

A "Private Limited" structure provides limited liability only when corporate and personal finances remain strictly segregated. **Failure to maintain statutory filings or commingling of funds** permits the judiciary to "pierce the veil," holding directors personally liable for all corporate debts and defaults.
