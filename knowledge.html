Founder's Legal Blueprint

01. Entity Selection Architecture.

LLP

Limited Liability Partnership

  • Pro Low compliance cost.
  • Con Not ideal for VC funding.

Private Limited

Recommended for Startups

  • Pro Highly fundable by VCs.
  • Con High compliance rigor.

Registered Partnership

Standard Business

  • Pro Easiest to setup.
  • Con Unlimited personal liability.

02. Formation Pipeline.

Step 01

Digital Identity (DSC & DIN)

Founders must secure a Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) and Director Identification Number (DIN) as the primary legal authentication.

Step 02

Name Approval & MOA/AOA

Drafting the Charter of the company (Memorandum and Articles of Association) to define business scope and bylaws.

Step 03

Certificate of Incorporation

Final approval from ROC (Registrar of Companies) awarding your COI and Corporate Identity Number (CIN).
